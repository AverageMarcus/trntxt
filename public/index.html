Presenting mtrain: a data-friendly train times site designed for phones on GPRS.

Usage

  • /dep/station - Departures from specified station
  • /dep/station1/station2 - Departures from station 1, calling at station 2
  • /arr/station - Arrivals at specified station
  • /arr/station1/station2 - Arrivals at station 1 which have previously called at station 2

About

  • No pictures or styling mean fast load times, even on 2G connections.
  • No JavaScript means any device can use it.
  • Tiny responses mean the site can be used even on the tiniest of data plans.
